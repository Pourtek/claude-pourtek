# Pourtek Company Knowledge

## Company Overview

Pourtek is a self-serve beverage tap system built for bars, restaurants, stadiums, hotels, golf courses, grocery stores, universities, residential communities, entertainment venues, airports, and other high-volume beverage environments.

Pourtek helps operators serve drinks faster, reduce waste, lower labor pressure, improve guest experience, and manage beverage programs with better visibility and control.

Customers tap an RFID card, wristband, or supported mobile credential, select a beverage, pour their own drink, and are charged by the ounce.

Pourtek was founded by bar owners to solve real hospitality problems: staff shortages, beverage loss, long lines, slow service, and poor operational visibility.

## Product Configuration Details

### Tekerator Configurations

Tekerators are portable, stand-alone self-serve beverage dispensing stations.

Available Tekerator faucet configurations:

- 2 faucets
- 4 faucets
- 6 faucets
- 8 faucets
- 10 faucets

Tekerators always use 2 faucets per customer-facing screen.

Examples:

- 2-faucet Tekerator = 1 screen
- 4-faucet Tekerator = 2 screens
- 6-faucet Tekerator = 3 screens
- 8-faucet Tekerator = 4 screens
- 10-faucet Tekerator = 5 screens

When writing or designing Tekerator materials, do not describe Tekerators as having random faucet/screen combinations. Always keep the rule: 2 faucets per screen.

### Tap Wall Configurations

Tap Walls are fixed self-serve tap installations.

Tap Walls always use 2 faucets per customer-facing screen.

Tap Walls are customizable and can be built with as many faucets as the venue wants, based on the operator’s layout, beverage program, and installation needs.

Examples:

- 2 wall taps = 1 screen
- 4 wall taps = 2 screens
- 6 wall taps = 3 screens
- 8 wall taps = 4 screens
- 10 wall taps = 5 screens
- Larger custom tap walls follow the same 2 faucets per screen rule

When writing or designing Tap Wall materials, position them as customizable fixed installations for venues that want a permanent self-serve beverage wall.


### Tap Walls

Fixed self-serve tap walls installed along bar or venue walls. Tap walls are ideal for high-volume locations that want a permanent self-serve beverage experience.

### Tekerator

Portable, stand-alone, all-in-one beverage dispensing station. Tekerator is useful for venues that want self-serve beverage service without major construction.

### Cloud Dashboard

Cloud-based dashboard for real-time reporting, inventory visibility, multi-location management, and operational control.

### Guest UI Screens

Customer-facing touchscreen displays that show beverage details, pricing, branding, imagery, animations, keg levels, and pour information.

### Staff Handheld

Android handheld device used by staff to create tabs, scan IDs, assign RFID cards or wristbands, monitor faucets, manage kegs, authorize customers, run reports, and control the self-pour operation from anywhere in the venue.

## How Pourtek Works

1. Staff creates a tab using the handheld, POS integration, RFID card, wristband, or supported customer credential.
2. Customer taps their RFID card, wristband, or supported credential at the tap.
3. The screen validates the customer and activates the beverage selection.
4. Customer pours their own drink.
5. Pourtek tracks ounces, servings, and cost in real time.
6. The customer’s tab updates automatically.
7. Staff can monitor activity, manage limits, change kegs, and review reporting.

## Key Differentiators

### Cloud-Based Architecture

Pourtek is designed as a cloud-based system using Salesforce and AWS infrastructure for scalable, real-time reporting and multi-location management.

### Handheld-First Operations

Pourtek staff are not tied to a fixed desktop station. The Android handheld acts as a staff command center for tabs, RFID setup, age verification, receipts, keg changes, reports, and customer authorization.

### RFID and Tap-to-Pour Experience

Customers use RFID cards, wristbands, or supported mobile credentials to activate taps and pour beverages themselves.

### Real-Time Reporting

Pourtek tracks pour volume, servings, transaction data, inventory, and customer activity in real time.

### Responsible-Service Controls

Pourtek supports consumption limits, staff re-authorization, customer suspension, and tracking by both ounces and servings.

### Brandable Customer Screens

Customer-facing screens can show branded backgrounds, beverage imagery, price per ounce, ABV, descriptions, keg levels, and animated visual elements.

### Multi-Location Management

Pourtek is built for operators that need visibility and control across more than one location.

### Beverage Flexibility

Pourtek can support beer, wine, cocktails, kombucha, coffee, THC-infused beverages where legal, non-alcoholic beverages, and mixed-use faucet configurations.

### POS Integration

Pourtek can integrate with systems such as GoTab when a venue wants to keep its existing POS workflow.
